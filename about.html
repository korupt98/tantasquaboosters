Our Mission

The Tantasqua Athletic Booster Club is a non-profit volunteer organization that exists to support student athletes and assist in the enhancement of the athletic program.

Our goal is to promote a spirit of sportsmanship and enthusiasm among the students and supporters of the Tantasqua Regional High School athletic program.

Recent Contributions

Some of our most recent contributions to the Tantasqua Athletic Program include:

  • Weight room equipment
  • Cameras for football
  • Baseball and softball pitching machines
  • Range finders for the golf teams
  • Scholarships awarded to graduating student athletes

The Tantasqua Athletic Program is one of the finest in the area. Please consider becoming a member of the Booster Club today. This is a wonderful opportunity to meet other dedicated individuals, and join in a worthwhile endeavor to support our student athletes.
