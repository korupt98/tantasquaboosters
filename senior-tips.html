Senior Year Pro Tips

Helpful advice for senior student athletes

Senior year is an exciting and busy time! Here are some tips for student athletes.

Stay Organized

Between practices, games, college apps, and academics, use a planner to track deadlines.

College Recruiting

Start early! Reach out to coaches, attend showcases, and prepare your highlight reels.

Enjoy the Journey

This is your last year as a Warrior. Soak in every moment — practices, games, friendships.

Apply for Scholarships

Apply for the Booster Club scholarship! Talk to your coach for more information.
